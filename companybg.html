Company Background

SmartTech Appliances was founded in the year 2025 through a college brainstorming project by a group of IT students who dreamed of starting an online business. What began as a simple class idea has grown into a trusted e-commerce store offering innovative and reliable household appliances for every home. The company operates through its official website and mobile app, allowing customers to easily browse, compare, and order products anytime, anywhere. All transactions, from product selection and payment to delivery tracking, are handled online with multiple payment options such as secure online payment or cash on delivery, ensuring a safe, hassle-free, and convenient shopping experience for everyone.
